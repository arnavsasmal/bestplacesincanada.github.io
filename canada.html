The best places in Canada

Best Places to visit in Canada
Niagra Falls

Niagra Falls is probably the best waterfall you will ever see. The best activity there is the hornblower ride which will take you right up to the waterfall.
CN Tower
CN tower is Canada's tallest building. There is a glass florr on top from where you can see the world below you while standing on it.
Toronto Zoo
Toronto Zoo is one of the world's most known zoos. It has a gigantic spectrum of animals ranging from snakes to pandas.
Quebec City
Quebec City may be the capital of the Quebec province in eastern Canada, but its French heritage, architecture and language make it appear more like a charming European village.
Best Provinces to Visit
Ontario

Ontario is a province in east-central Canada that borders the U.S. and the Great Lakes.
Nova Scotia
Nova Scotia is one of eastern Canada's Maritime provinces on the Atlantic. Consisting of a peninsula and offshore islands, it's home to puffins and seals.
